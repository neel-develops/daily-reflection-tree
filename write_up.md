# Design Rationale

## Question Design
Questions were designed to feel natural and situational rather than evaluative. Each question reflects real workplace scenarios to encourage honest selection.

## Branching Logic
Branching is based on:
- Internal vs External (Axis 1)
- Contribution vs Entitlement (Axis 2)
- Self vs Others (Axis 3)

Each branch leads to different reflective statements without judgment.

## Psychological Basis
- Locus of Control (Rotter)
- Growth Mindset (Dweck)
- Organizational Citizenship Behavior
- Self-Transcendence (Maslow)

## Trade-offs
- Fixed options limit nuance but ensure determinism
- Simplified signals instead of complex scoring

## Improvements
- Add longitudinal tracking
- Personalized reflection history
- Better summary synthesis
